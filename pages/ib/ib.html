Challenge
To design a platform that lets us manage queries/questions raised by different users about InterviewBit Academy efficiently so that our users get relevant answers/solutions on time.
Research
The main objective of my research phase was to answer the following questions :
  • How do other companies/softwares tackle this problem?
  • What are the factors that need to be focussed on to improve the speed at which issues can be resolved?
  • What are the factors that need to be focused on to improve customer satisfaction?
  • What tools/techniques can I use to automate parts of this process, incorporate feedback for continual improvement and reduce the load on the operations team.
    Here are my key findings from my research:
  • Nelson Study showed that complaints went down 30% by utilizing text analytics and identifying key themes.
  • Softwares like ZenDesk use macros(pre-prepared responses) to respond quickly to customer requests
  • Softwares like Salesforce have auto response rules that allow for automatic responses to questions that fit a particular theme.
  • Sites like Google and LinkedIn give Auto-suggestions for messages and emails received, allowing for faster responses.â€™

Design Decisions
Some of the design decisions i took and why.
Categories from Keyword analytics and FAQs
Categorization helps with making the pre-recorded response work better and also makes the process of identifying tasks that need to be reassigned much faster.
Sending an initial email to reduce the number of issues
The e-mail can help reduce the number of tickets generated by reducing straightforward queries.
Auto Assigning
Auto-assigning issues as opposed to picking up issues one by one as auto assigning prioritizes speed. However, if one member is not available on a particular day, it may result in extra complexities to go to settings and deactivate the member for the day.
Auto-assigning unassigned tasks
If a member is too occupied with a certain task and he can't finish it, he can un-assign it himself and the issue will be shifted to a member with the least amount of tasks.
Fullscreen vs Splitscreen
A split screen allows for a much faster response and resolution time in exchange for viewing a lesser amount of issues per screen.

Other decisions like not making the filters sticky have been made considering the frequency of use of such filters.
